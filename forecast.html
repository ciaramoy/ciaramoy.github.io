Welcome to the weather forecast.
From the name of this page you might assume that you will find out weather conditions in advance.
Not a bad conclusion since the word forecast is comprised of "cast" and "fore", so you can say you are going to cast a or predict an outcome before hand.
So yes. You are right. This page should show you future conditions. However you forget one essential truth.
This is the future.
This is the time which brings forth infinite outcomes. And with each passing moment your actions cement these outcomes into one definitive memory.
It is for this reason you will only be told about the physical state around you now.
So with your sense of discernment, taking into context what outcomes are possible now, it is hoped you take great action in crafting your future
…

Or perhaps forget.
Forget the constraints of reality.
After all, the only proof of reality is within your mind. Therefore, your only constraints exist only if you think it does.
So forget them.
Perhaps certain realities are unattainable from where you stand. Yet,
if you move a little to the left,
or perhaps a little to the right,
or even a few feet forward,
or maybe if you take a step back
you might be closer to the reality you seek.
A change in perspective is all you need.
Now take control, whatever you think it to be, let it be,
This is the most accurate forecast because it's what you believe,
or perhaps simply a digital representation of pathetic fallacy,
Either way you are in charge of what you see.
